Our Academic Programs

Preschool (Creche, Nursery, KG)

Our preschool program provides a nurturing environment where young children develop foundational skills through play-based learning, creative activities and social interaction.

Program Highlights:

  • Age-appropriate learning activities
  • Language and literacy development
  • Numeracy and problem-solving skills
  • Creative arts and music

Daily Schedule:

  • 7:30 AM - Arrival & Free Play
  • 8:30 AM - Circle Time
  • 9:00 AM - Learning Centers
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch & Nap Time

"Our preschool teachers are specially trained in early childhood development to ensure your child receives the best possible start to their educational journey."

Primary (Basic 1 to 6)

Our primary program builds on foundational skills while introducing more structured learning in core subjects aligned with the Ghana Education Service curriculum.

Curriculum Overview:

Subject Focus Areas
English Language Reading, Writing, Grammar, Comprehension
Mathematics Numeracy, Problem Solving, Geometry
Science Environmental Studies, Basic Physics & Chemistry
Social Studies History, Geography, Citizenship
Ghanaian Language Twi, Ga or Ewe (Parent's choice)

Extracurricular Activities:

  • Sports (Football, Athletics)
  • Music and Dance
  • Art and Craft
  • STEM Club

Assessment:

  • Continuous Assessment (30%)
  • Mid-Term Exams (20%)
  • End of Term Exams (50%)

Admissions

Admission Process

Submit a completed application form along with the following documents:

  • Birth certificate (photocopy)
  • Two passport-sized photographs
  • Previous school report (if applicable)
  • Medical report

Depending on the class level, students may be required to:

  • Take an assessment test (Primary & JHS)
  • Participate in an interactive session (Preschool)
  • Attend an interview with parents (All levels)

Parents will be notified of the admission decision within 5 working days after the assessment/interview. Successful applicants will receive an admission letter with details about fees, uniforms, and other requirements.

To complete enrollment, parents must:

  • Pay the required fees
  • Submit all outstanding documents
  • Purchase school uniforms and supplies
  • Attend orientation session
